Mosberger-Tang, Egbert Eich, and Freescale Semiconductor, Inc.

what we inherit.

The original U-Boot drivers/bios_emulator/ also contained four
GPLv2-only or no-licence-header wrapper files (besys.c,
atibios.c, vesa.c, vesa.h) and three SciTech-licensed but
unused glue files (bios.c, biosemu.c, biosemui.h). None of
those was ever compiled into the Pegasos II firmware; they were
removed from this tree on 2026-04-26 to keep the source bundle
free of GPL-licensed code and to make this license-summary
accurate.

Clean-room note
---------------

U-Boot is a Denx-led project (denx.de) with no lineage from the
bPlan/CodeGen Pegasos II proprietary firmware. The x86emu library
inside it derives from SciTech's 1996-era realmode emulator, which
predates the Pegasos II by years. Vendoring it into this rewrite
introduces no cross-contamination with the original Pegasos II
binary.
